## Slide 4: CRITICAL Priority #1 - Full Data Export

**The Problem:**
- No comprehensive export for full assessment history
- External researchers cannot access data for analysis
- Missing data dictionary/codebook

**The Solution:**
- Wide-format CSV with one row per program
- All assessment cycles with baseline and improved ratings
- Goal tracking across assessments
- Format optimized for SPSS, R, Stata

**Impact:** Essential for evaluation work and demonstrating measurable impact to funders

---

## Slide 5: CRITICAL Priority #2 - Assessment History

**The Problem:**
- System doesn't support multiple assessments per program
- Programs approaching 3-year mark need second assessments ⏰
- Cannot view past assessments or track progress over time

**The Solution:**
- Support multiple assessments (only 1 active at a time)
- Clear workflow for closing and opening assessments
- Historical access with side-by-side comparison tools
- Progress charts showing improvement over time

**Impact:** Critical for tracking program progress and demonstrating improvements
